  • What Does it Mean To Be Made in the Image of Christ?

    I read an amazing book for my Vineyard Institute Biblical Metanarrative class called The Biblical Metanarrative: One God, One Plan, One Story by my late professor, Bill Jackson. Near the end, he includes a long, anonymous quote which just takes my breath away. It’s about what it means to be made into the image of Christ. As you will see, this takes death to self, spiritually speaking. Before you read this, ask yourself if you live for yourself or for God or both.
