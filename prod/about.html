Kristy Lahoda

About Kristy

I live in the tension between being redeemed by Jesus yet still imperfect.

“To keep me from becoming conceited because of these surpassingly great revelations, there was given me a thorn in my flesh, a messenger of Satan, to torment me. Three times I pleaded with the Lord to take it away from me. But he said to me, “My grace is sufficient for you, for my power is made perfect in weakness.’ Therefore, I will boast all the more gladly about my weaknesses, so that Christ’s power may rest on me. That is why, for Christ’s sake, I delight in weaknesses, in insults, in hardships, in persecutions, in difficulties. For when I am weak, then I am strong.” The Bible, 2 Corinthians 12:7-10

I am in recovery from anorexia, a 22 year off-and-on battle. It has tried to rob me of life and has stolen years, but my soul is God’s. My body has recovered and is recovering. I’ve overcome and am overcoming by the Holy Spirit, whom directs me to God. I cannot heal myself. Only God can.

Jesus, the Word, became flesh and came to give us life and life abundantly. My desire is to share my journey of God’s love through my words, whether written or spoken. Whether you struggle with an eating disorder, identity, addictions, or self-hatred, know someone who does, or just want to know more about God’s love and power, I welcome you to follow along on this wild journey. I am learning to live, really live.
